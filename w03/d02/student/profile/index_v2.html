Kamari Aykes is a Bloomfield, NJ native and currently serves as Secretary of the National Urban League Young Professionals. She joined the Portland, Oregon chapter in 2011, under the leadership of the late Robert K. Ingram, and later served as President. Ms. Aykes is an alumna of Rutgers University-Newark, with a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry. In addition to her commitment to service and empowerment in the Movement, she was most recently employed by Oregon Health & Science University's department of Behavioral Neuroscience. The outcome of her latest project on the ADHD population was recently featured in the Huffington Post. Her duties also included serving as co-director of the Youth Engaged in Science (YES!) Initiative, a S.T.E.M.-focused educational outreach program. The initiative aims to teach underrepresented youth about their developing brains and non-traditional career opportunities in these targeted fields. Moving forward, she hopes to combine her skills and interests in policy, community engagement, and STEM to be an influential advocate for those in need.
